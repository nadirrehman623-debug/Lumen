Welcome to Lumen!

Lumen is an AI based platform that allows students to Learn new topics via question based learning. Instead of handing the answer, students are prompted to think critically and apply their knowledge. Lumen is designed to ask thought-provoking questions. Lumen encourages students to engage with the material and develop a deeper understanding of the subject matter. Lumen is a great tool for students who want to learn new topics or deepen their understanding of existing ones, And Create interdiciplinary intuitions.

  • Lumen currently supports a wide range of subjects. like:
  • Math
  • Philosophy
  • Computer Science
  • Biology
  • Chemistry
  • Physics
  • History
  • Literature
  • Linguistics

To get started, please register for an account or log in if you already have one.
